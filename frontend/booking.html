Frequently Asked Questions

How much advance payment is required?

+

We require 30% advance payment to confirm your booking. The remaining 70% is due on the event day.

Can I customize the package?

+

Yes! We offer customizable packages based on your specific needs. Contact us for a personalized quote.

How long does it take to deliver photos/videos?

+

Photos are delivered within 15 days, and videos within 30 days. We also provide same-day previews for selected packages.

Do you travel outside Maharashtra?

+

Yes, we travel across India and internationally. Travel and accommodation charges apply for outstation events.
